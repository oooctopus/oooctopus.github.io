Looking for Students

We are always looking for highly motivated and energetic graduate/undergraduate students to work on research topics related to data analytics, machine learning, marine object detection and recognition, and underwater wireless communications. Candidates are expected to have solid background in signal processing, wireless communications, and/or machine learning. Passion, hard work, and strong motivation towards academic excellence should be necessary, while strong mathematical background and/or hands-on system implementation is a big plus. If interested and have the right background, please email us at yuzhouli@hust.edu.cn.
