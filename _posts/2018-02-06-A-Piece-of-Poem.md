> Poems of Rabindranath Tagore

# Today's Poem
<center> <mark><b><i>If you shed tears when you miss the sun, you also miss the stars</i></b></mark> </center> 

&ensp;&ensp;这句诗出自泰戈尔的《飞鸟集》，意为“如果你因失去太阳而流泪，那么你也将错过群星”。
